# Backlink Outreach Email Templates

## General Guidelines

- Keep emails under 150 words
- Personalize every email
- Provide value first
- Make it easy for them to say yes
- Follow up 2-3 times over 2 weeks
- Use the recipient's name
- Mention their content specifically

---

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Personalize with recipient's name
- Mention value proposition
- Use numbers when relevant
- Create curiosity without being clickbait

### Body Tips
- Start with a personalized greeting
- Mention their content specifically
- Provide value first, ask second
- Keep it under 150 words
- Include clear call-to-action
- Add signature with credentials

### Follow-Up Tips
- Space follow-ups 3-4 days apart
- Add new value each time
- Keep it brief
- Don't be pushy
- Know when to stop (after 2-3 follow-ups)

### Tracking Tips
- Use email tracking to monitor opens/clicks
- Tag emails by campaign
- Track response rates
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor which templates work best
