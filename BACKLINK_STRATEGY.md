### 3. Broken Link Building (10% of backlinks)

#### Process
1. Find broken links on relevant pages using:
   - Ahrefs Broken Link Checker
   - SEMrush
   - Check My Links (Chrome extension)

2. Create replacement content for broken resources

3. Reach out to site owners with your replacement

#### Outreach Email Template
```
Subject: Found a broken link on [Page Name]

Hi [Name],

I was browsing your page about [Topic] and found a broken link:
[Broken Link URL]

I noticed you were linking to [Old Resource], which seems to be down.

I actually wrote a comprehensive guide on [Topic] that might be a good replacement:
[Your Content URL]

Would you be open to updating the link?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## Backlink Quality Guidelines

### Good Backlinks
- ✅ From relevant, authoritative sites (DA 30+)
- ✅ Natural anchor text (branded, URL, or descriptive)
- ✅ Contextual links within content
- ✅ From sites with organic traffic
- ✅ Follow links (though nofollow is okay for diversity)
- ✅ From diverse domains (not all from same site)
- ✅ From pages with content relevant to AI tools

### Bad Backlinks
- ❌ From spammy or low-quality sites
- ❌ Exact-match keyword anchor text
- ❌ Sitewide footer or sidebar links
- ❌ From private blog networks (PBNs)
- ❌ Paid links (without nofollow)
- ❌ From irrelevant sites
- ❌ From sites with thin or duplicate content

## Outreach Best Practices

### Email Outreach Tips
1. **Personalize every email** - Use recipient's name, mention their content
2. **Keep it short** - Under 150 words
3. **Provide value first** - Explain why your content helps their readers
4. **Make it easy** - Provide the exact link and suggested anchor text
5. **Follow up** - 2-3 follow-ups over 2 weeks
6. **Build relationships** - Don't just ask for links, engage with their content

### Follow-Up Schedule
- **Day 1:** Initial outreach
- **Day 4:** First follow-up
- **Day 8:** Second follow-up
- **Day 12:** Final follow-up

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Buying backlinks** - Can lead to penalties
2. **Spammy outreach** - Personalize and provide value
3. **Low-quality content** - Only create high-quality, valuable content
4. **Ignoring relevance** - Only target relevant sites
5. **Over-optimizing anchor text** - Keep it natural
6. **Not following up** - Follow up 2-3 times
7. **Building too fast** - Natural link velocity is 5-10 links per week
8. **Ignoring lost backlinks** - Monitor and reclaim lost links
